Services

What can I expect at an in person Reiki session?*

Reiki promotes a deep sense of relaxation. It is within this relaxed state that the body is best able to heal. It is also a wonderful time for the mind to unwind. We will begin with a brief interview and a client intake form. This helps me make the best use of our time. Rest assured your confidentiality is maintained at all times. An in person Reiki session takes place on a massage table while you are fully clothed. An established pattern of hand placements on the body are followed. You will be informed of this pattern prior to the session. If you are uncomfortable lying down or with the hand placements on the body accommodations are easily made without affecting treatment results. The priority is to make you comfortable. This is where the restoration can begin.

What can I expect at a Distance/Absentee Reiki appointment?*

A Reiki session can be provided at a distance. That means that I can be in one place and the receiver can be in another. Distance Reiki (also known as Absentee Reiki) is just as effective as an in person treatment. The person receiving the energy can be working, sleeping, or relaxing - it does not matter. However, I prefer that the client takes time to relax because often they can feel the energy transfer as if they were having a hands on treatment!

If your choice is a distance appointment we will begin by meeting via email, text, or phone (your preference). During this time we will discover your needs and what you hope to receive from Reiki. We will set up an appointment tailored to you and have a check in following your appointment to let you know what I felt and to answer any questions.

Distance Reiki is a great way to support a friend or family member who is in the hospital or otherwise immobile. Permission from the individual receiving Reiki is always required unless it is an emergency. No distance is too great. I have clients in many states across the U.S. and internationally as well. I have been doing these treatments for many years and to this day it still leaves me a bit awestruck.

Reiki for Groups and Businesses*

Any group, no matter how large or small, can receive Reiki as long as there is a commonality. The more the members of the group have in common the stronger the Reiki. The process is called Reiki Group Stacking. It can be as simple supporting a small group project or sending Reiki to a global crisis. Businesses and other endeavors can also be the benefactor of Reiki.

When is a good time to consider Reiki for pets?*

  • When bringing a new pet home.
  • When kenneling to reduce anxiety.
  • When sick or injured to calm and reduce pain.
  • When transitioning for comfort and peace.

* Please Note

* It's important to note that Reiki energy is received uniquely by each individual. Some see immediate changes. For others it may take several sessions. Still others see no change. There are no guarantees with Reiki.
