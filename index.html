Reiki is a healing art of Japanese origin. Its name comes from the Japanese words rei which means universal life and ki which means energy. This universal life energy flows all around us and through every living thing. It enables plants to grow, broken bones to mend, and emotions to calm. It is within us all and we all use Reiki every day. When you hold the hand of a frightened child, give a hug or put your hand over a toothache to soothe the pain, that is Reiki. Specially trained Reiki practitioners are attuned to increase this flow of energy and to transfer it to their clients to restore, unblock, and revitalize energy flow. Reiki enhances the body's natural healing response. Treatments are safe, gentle, and effective and are suitable for everyone, including children, seniors and pets.
