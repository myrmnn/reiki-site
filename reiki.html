Reiki

Reiki is a healing art of Japanese origin. lts name comes from the Japanese words "rei" which means universal life and "ki" which means energy. lt is the energy that flows all around us and through every living thing. lt enables plants to grow, broken bones to mend, and emotions to stabilize. lt is inherent in us all and we all use Reiki every day. When you hold the hand of a frightened child, give a hug or put your hand over a toothache, that is Reiki. Specially trained Reiki practitioners are attuned to increase this flow of energy and to transfer it to their clients to restore, unblock, and revitalize energy flow. lt enhances the body's natural healing response. Treatments are safe, gentle, and effective and are suitable for everyone, including children and seniors.

Why Reiki?
What can it do for me?

It is believed that Reiki can accelerate healing on the physical, mental and emotional levels therefore it can help just about any condition. Many use Reiki to lessen the side effects of chemotherapy and alcohol/drug withdrawal. What can I expect at a Reiki session?

Reiki promotes a deep sense of relaxation. lt is within this relaxed state that the body is best able to heal. lt ii also a wonderful time for the mind to unwind. We will begin with a brief interview and a client intake form. This helps me make the best use of our time. Rest assured your confidentiality is maintained at all times. A Reiki session takes place on a massage table while you are fully clothed. An established pattern of hand placements on the body are followed. You witll be informed of this pattern prior to the session. lf you are uncomfortable lying down or with the hand placements on the body accommodations are easily made without effecting treatment results. The priority is to make you comfortable. This is where the restoration can begin.

Distance/Absentee Reiki

A Reiki session can be provided at a distance. That means that I can be in one place and the receiver can be in another. Distance or Absentee Reiki is just as effective as an in person treatment. The person receiving the energy can be working, sleeping, or relaxing, it does not matter. However, I prefer that the client takes time to relax because often they can feel the energy transfer as if they were having a hands on treatment! This is a great way to support a friend or family member who is in the hospital or otherwise immobile. Permission from the individual receiving Reiki is required unless it is an emergency. I have clients in many states across the U.S. I even have international clients. I have been doing these treatments for many years and to this day it still leaves me a bit awestruck.
